Chicken Parmesan

Description

Follow instructions below for a delicious homecooked Italian meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 3 large chicken breasts halved
  • 1 cup Panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 430 degrees F.
  2. Whisk together eggs, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish.
  3. When chicken is ready for cooking, mix bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, and garlic powder together in a separate bowl.
  4. Fry chicken until golden and crispy.
  5. Place chicken on prepared baking tray and top with sauce.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
